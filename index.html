How It Works

  • Each Camp Ambassador commits to raising $2,250 — the exact one-time cost of creating a custom production mold for that camp toy.
  • The mold is made once and can be reused in the future — this is not a recurring charge.
  • Once a family officially joins, we begin mold design and production. Estimated timeline: ~6 weeks.
  • When the factory is ready, the full $2,250 must be available from donations and/or the family's own payment.
