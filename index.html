Our Mission

We know what camp gave us.
Now we're building something to pass it on.

The Squeeze Project is a kid-led initiative built on a simple belief: every child deserves the opportunity to experience summer camp.

We've partnered with summer camps and the national charity SCOPE to create custom squeeze toys inspired by today's most popular kid-loved trend. Each toy is designed for a specific camp — and every dollar of profit goes toward sending kids to camp.

Our goal is simple: build something that helps more kids get to camp — now and every year going forward.

Someone we deeply respect once told us: "Camp is the gift." Those words took on many meanings for us over the years. Most importantly — we want to pass that gift on.

The Bigger Picture

We're in the first inning.
The best is still ahead.

Right now we're funding the molds — a one-time cost per camp. Once that's done, the real engine begins.

Phase 1 — Now
Fund the Molds

Ambassadors fund a one-time mold for each camp's custom toy. Once made, it never needs to be made again.

Phase 2 — Next
Sell the Toys

Each camp's custom toy goes into production and starts selling — at camp stores, online, and through retail. Every toy sold helps fund the program.

Phase 3 — Always
Self-Sustaining

No more fundraising. Toy sales fund camp scholarships permanently — sending more kids to camp every single year, without needing to fundraise again.
