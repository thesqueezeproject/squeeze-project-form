Our Mission

We know what camp gave us. Now we're paying it forward.

The Squeeze Project is a kid-led charitable initiative built on a simple belief: every child deserves the opportunity to experience summer camp.

We've partnered with summer camps and the national charity SCOPE to create custom, squeeze toys inspired by today's most viral kid-loved toy trend. Each toy is designed specifically for a participating camp — and every dollar of profit goes directly toward sending kids to camp.

The toys themselves? The same satisfying squish you already know and love — now custom-made for your camp. Premium slow-rise compound, built to last, and endlessly squeezable.

Our goal is simple: help as many kids as possible get to camp — now and in the future.

🏕️

100% of Profits

Every dollar earned from toy sales goes toward funding camp experiences for kids who need a little extra help getting there.

How It Works

From commitment to camp — here's the journey

A Camp Ambassador raises $2,250, we build the toy, camps sell it, kids go to camp. Simple.

1
🤝

A Camp Joins

A camp partners with The Squeeze Project. A family commits to raising $2,250 — the one-time mold cost for their custom toy.

2
✏️

The Toy Is Designed

We create a custom squeeze toy unique to the camp. The mold is made once — it can be used again and again in the future.

3
🛍️

Toys Are Sold

Sold on our website, in camp stores, and through select retailers. Every purchase directly supports the program.

4
⛺

Kids Go to Camp

100% of profits fund camp experiences for kids who need a little extra help getting there. That's the whole point.

Camp Ambassador Program

Be the reason a kid
gets to go.

Each Camp Ambassador commits to raising $2,250 — the exact one-time cost to create a custom mold for their camp's squeeze toy. Once it's made, it never needs to be made again.

🎯

Commit to the Goal

Raise $2,250 through friends, family, and community — or cover it directly. You set the pace.

📣

Spread the Word

Share your campaign with your community. The more people who know, the more kids who go to camp.

✏️

Approve the Design

When we present your camp's toy design, we ask for your feedback and approval within 5 business days.

📞

Stay in Touch

Be available for a quick check-in during the ~6 week production process. We'll keep you posted every step.

What We Ask of Our Ambassadors

  • ✓ Commit to raising $2,250 through friends, family, and community — or cover it directly
  • ✓ Share your camp's campaign with your network and help spread the word
  • ✓ Review and approve your camp's custom toy design within 5 business days when presented
  • ✓ Be available for a brief check-in during the ~6-week design and production process
  • ✓ Ensure the full $2,250 is available when the factory payment is due
