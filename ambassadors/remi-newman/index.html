Remy's Story

Tyler Hill Camp has shaped who Remy is. The summers, the friendships, the moments that only camp can create — Remy knows firsthand what it means to belong somewhere special.


As a Founding Ambassador for The Squeeze Project, Remy is raising $2,250 to create a custom squeeze toy designed just for Tyler Hill Camp. The mold gets made once — and every toy sold from it helps send more kids to camp, forever.


"Camp is the gift." Help Remy pass it on. 🏕️
