Parker's Story

Camp Pontiac has been one of the most important parts of Parker's life. The friendships, the memories, the experiences that only summer camp can give — she wants every kid to have that chance.


As a Founding Ambassador of The Squeeze Project, Parker is raising $2,250 to create a one-of-a-kind custom squeeze toy designed just for Camp Pontiac. The mold gets made once, and every toy sold from it goes toward sending more kids to camp — forever.


"Camp is the gift." Help Parker pass it on. 🏕️
