Jordana's Story

Camp Westmont has shaped who Jordana is. The summers, the friendships, the moments that only camp can create — Jordana knows firsthand what it means to belong somewhere special.


As a Founding Ambassador for The Squeeze Project, Jordana is raising $2,250 to create a custom squeeze toy designed just for Camp Westmont. The mold gets made once — and every toy sold from it helps send more kids to camp, forever.
