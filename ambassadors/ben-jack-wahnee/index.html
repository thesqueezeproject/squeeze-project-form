Ben & Jack's Story

Camp Wah-Nee has shaped who Ben and Jack are. The summers, the friendships, the moments that only camp can create — they know firsthand what it means to belong somewhere special.


As a Founding Ambassador for The Squeeze Project, Ben & Jack are raising $3,000 to create a custom squeeze toy designed just for Camp Wah-Nee. The mold gets made once — and every toy sold from it helps send more kids to camp, forever.


"Camp is the gift." Help Ben & Jack pass it on. 🏕️
