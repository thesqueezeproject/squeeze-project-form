Ben's Story

Camp Wah-Nee has shaped who Ben is. The summers, the friendships, the moments that only camp can create — Ben knows firsthand what it means to belong somewhere special.


As a Founding Ambassador for The Squeeze Project, Ben is raising $2,250 to create a custom squeeze toy designed just for Camp Wah-Nee. The mold gets made once — and every toy sold from it helps send more kids to camp, forever.
