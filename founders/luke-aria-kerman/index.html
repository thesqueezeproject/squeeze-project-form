Luke & Aria's Story

Camp IHC has been one of the most important parts of Luke and Aria's lives. The friendships, the memories, the experiences that only summer camp can give — they want every kid to have that chance.


As Founders of The Squeeze Project, Luke and Aria are raising $2,250 together to create a one-of-a-kind custom squeeze toy designed just for Camp IHC. The mold gets made once, and every toy sold from it goes toward sending more kids to camp — forever.


"Camp is the gift." Help Luke & Aria pass it on. 🏕️
